Description

This is a simple pasta dish, perfect for busy families who want to enjoy Vegemite in a new way.
Add cheese, and vegetables of your liking to give it that final touch.

Ingredients

  • Vegemite, 1.5 tsp
  • Pasta, 100 g
  • Butter, 1 tbsp
  • Broccoli, 1 cup
  • Onion, 0.5 chopped
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Chop onion and broccoli, then set aside
  2. Add pasta, onion and broccoli to boiling water
  3. Melt butter and vegemite into pasta, stirring frequently until cooked
  4. Strain pasta water, and serve
